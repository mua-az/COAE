
To detect networks anomaly we are gonna use decision trees
Random forest uses nested decision trees for better prediction, it may be of the following types

**--> Bootstrapping --** Multiple subsets of the training data are created via sampling with replacement. Each subset trains a separate decision tree.
**--> Tree construction --** For each tree, a random subset of features is considered at every split
**--> Voting --** The final outcome is decided by the votes of each tree in forest
